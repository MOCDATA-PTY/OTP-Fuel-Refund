Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if my business qualifies for fuel tax refunds?

Businesses that use fuel for off-road equipment, refrigeration units, power generation, and other non-highway applications may qualify for fuel tax refunds. Industries like agriculture, construction, manufacturing, and transportation often have significant refund potential.

How much does your service cost?

Our service operates on a contingency basis—this means there are no upfront fees. We only get paid a percentage of the refunds we recover for you. If we don't secure a refund, you don't pay anything.

How long does it take to receive a refund?

The timeline varies by state and the complexity of your claim, but most clients receive their refunds within 6-12 weeks of submission. We'll provide you with regular updates throughout the process.
