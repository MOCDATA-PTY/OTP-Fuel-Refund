Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to provide fuel tax refund services:

  • Business name and contact information
  • Tax identification numbers
  • Fuel purchase records and receipts
  • Vehicle and mileage information
  • Financial documents related to your business

How We Use Information

Your information is used to:

  • Process fuel tax refund applications
  • Communicate with tax authorities on your behalf
  • Provide customer support and updates
  • Comply with legal requirements
  • Improve our services

Information Sharing

We may share your information with:

  • Tax authorities for refund processing
  • Professional service providers assisting your case
  • Legal authorities when required by law

We do not sell your personal information.

Data Security

We protect your information using industry-standard security measures including encryption, secure servers, and access controls.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request corrections to inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

Contact for Privacy Matters

For privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights, contact us at privacy@fuelrefundinstitute.com or (424) 222-5290.

Policy Updates

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted on our website with the effective date.
